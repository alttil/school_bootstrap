About me

I am a custom service orientated multitalent with over 10 years experience of coaching people. My clients include people with different backgrounds from students to top executives of the biggest Finnish brands e.g. Nokia, OP, Kone and Neste. My work is driven by the desire to inspire people to give 100% in sports. Last 4 years I worked as a Team Leader in intensive tennis camps to abroad and as a Head Coach of Junior camps in Finland. In our customer satisfaction surveys I always have been selected among the 3 best coaches. I work hard to give the client and my organization the best.

Now studying ICT in Haaga-Helia University of Applied Sciences. In the future my goal is to combine my previus experience from sports and my current studies from programming field.
